An introduction to State Development Organization-(SDO)

A non-governmental, non-religious, impartial and humanitarian organization, working for deprived, marginalized and socially excluded portions of the civil society. SDO has its office at Islamabad and Regional Offices in Swat, Muzaffargarh & Bannu. The organization was formed in February 1998 and was first registered under Societies Act 1860 (XXI 1860) in 2010. SOD was also registered with FATA Social Welfare Department in 2015, under Voluntary Social Welfare Agencies Ordinance 1961 (XLVI). SDO has been working with several international Organizations, donors & government on multi sector projects in different area of Pakistan.

Our Vission

‘Equal, just and sensible distribution of resources, it’s appropriate utilization and conservation so that a prosperous society develops on the principles of peace, justice and prosperity.’

Our Mission

To develop alternate leadership of socially excluded portions of the civil society from grass roots level and enhance their will power and livelihood, to take up responsibility by sharing of information and confirmation of participation in decision making process at all level through democratic methods for effective social context and speeding up social change”.
